Ridge gourd / Dodka / Turai simple 


1. Ingredients 
	Dodka
	Grind peanuts 
	oil
	muster seeds
	cumin seeds
	turmeric powder
	garlic
	salt
	red chilli powder
	coriander leaves
	coconut

2. Process
	1. Add cumin, mustered seeds, garlic, turmeric powder in hot oil
	2. Add dodka and keep stirring. Keep it on gas for 7-10 min
	3. Add red chili powder, peanuts powder, coconut and water and salt later
	4. Keep it for a while on gas
